Business and Management Program

The Business and Management program provides resources on economics, marketing, finance, organizational behavior, and strategic management. Ideal for students pursuing careers in business administration and entrepreneurship.

Program Overview

Program Overview: This course provides comprehensive training in business and management with a focus on both theoretical foundations and practical applications.

Learning Outcomes: Upon completion, students will have mastered the core concepts and developed practical skills applicable in real-world scenarios.

Assessment Methods Combination of exams, projects, and continuous assessments.

Career Opportunities: Graduates can pursue careers in various fields including software development, data analysis, system architecture, and more.
